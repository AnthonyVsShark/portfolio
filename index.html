00. Introduction

Originally from Phoenix, Arizona. The son of a single mother. I found my passion for art and technology from the very first moment I got my hands on my grandmothers barely functional Macintosh 2 at some point in the very late 80s or early 90s. Self taught web developer thanks to flash and myspace. Studied architecture, but chose digital design as my career path. Initially working with some small Arizona based design and development agencies. Then moving to New York City to join the product design team at Foursquare. Eventually relocating to San Francisco, where over the past decade I've helped design and ship features and products for companies such as Twitter, Slack, Airbnb and Github. Beyond work, I am an avid tattoo collector. Former professional musician. Father and husband.
